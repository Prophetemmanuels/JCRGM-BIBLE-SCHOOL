Pneumatology – Doctrine of the Holy Spirit

1. Personhood of the Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit is not a force or power but a Person—fully God.

Scripture: Acts 5:3–4

2. Work of the Holy Spirit

  • Conviction of sin
  • Regeneration
  • Sanctification
  • Empowerment

3. Baptism of the Holy Spirit

The baptism of the Holy Spirit empowers believers for witness and ministry.

Scripture: Acts 1:8

4. Gifts of the Holy Spirit

1 Corinthians 12

5. Fruit of the Spirit

Galatians 5:22–23

Doctrine Guardrail:
Gifts never replace character. Fruit must come before public ministry.
