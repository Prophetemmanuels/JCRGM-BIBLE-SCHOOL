Ordination & Commissioning Process

1. Eligibility

  • Completion of Year 3
  • Sound doctrine
  • Proven character
  • Faithful service

2. Levels of Ordination

Licensed Minister
Authorized to preach and assist in ministry.
Ordained Minister
Authorized to lead congregations and administer ordinances.
Commissioned Apostle / Prophet / Pastor / Evangelist / Teacher
Recognized by leadership based on calling, fruit, and doctrine.

3. Ordination Requirements

  • Oral doctrinal examination
  • Character review
  • Laying on of hands
  • Public affirmation

4. Ordination Vow

This ordination is lawfully and spiritually recognized by JESUS CHRIST REDEEMS GLOBAL MINISTRIES. Any claim of ordination without record in the official registry is null and void.
