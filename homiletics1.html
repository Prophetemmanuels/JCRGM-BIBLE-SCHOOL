Homiletics I – Foundations of Preaching

Homiletics I – Foundations of Preaching

1. Definition of Homiletics

Homiletics is the art and science of preparing and delivering sermons that faithfully communicate the Word of God under the guidance of the Holy Spirit.

2. The Source of Preaching

  • The Word of God (Scripture)
  • The Holy Spirit
  • The burden of God for His people

Key Scripture: 2 Timothy 4:2

3. The Call to Preach

Preaching is not a career—it is a divine calling.

Example:
Jeremiah did not volunteer—God called him (Jeremiah 1:5).

4. Christ-Centered Preaching

Every sermon must lead people to Christ, repentance, faith, or growth in Him.

Scripture: Luke 24:27

5. Types of Sermons

  • Textual Sermon
  • Topical Sermon
  • Expository Sermon

6. The Preacher’s Life

  • Prayer life
  • Holiness
  • Humility
  • Teachability
Warning:
A gifted preacher without character becomes dangerous to the Church.
