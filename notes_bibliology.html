Bibliology – Doctrine of Scripture

Bibliology – Doctrine of Scripture

1. Inspiration of Scripture

The Bible is God-breathed and authoritative.

Scripture: 2 Timothy 3:16

2. Authority of Scripture

The Bible is the final authority in faith, doctrine, and conduct.

3. Canon of Scripture

  • Old Testament – 39 books
  • New Testament – 27 books

4. Reliability of Scripture

Scripture is preserved by God and trustworthy.
