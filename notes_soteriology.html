Soteriology – Doctrine of Salvation

1. Meaning of Salvation

Salvation is deliverance from sin, death, and separation from God through Christ.

2. Repentance

Turning away from sin toward God.

Scripture: Acts 3:19

3. Faith

Trusting fully in Christ alone.

4. Justification

Declared righteous by God through faith.

5. Sanctification

Ongoing process of holiness.

6. Assurance of Salvation

Based on Christ, not emotions.

Assignment

Explain salvation using Romans 3–5.
